Data Transmission

This website is for anyone who is interested in data transmission GCSE.

There are two main types of data transmission.

This inculdes:

  • Parallel transmission
  • Serial transmission

    • Serial transmission

  • One bit is transferred at a time.
  • It is safer and more reliable.
  • It is also less expensive when transferring bits at a long distance.

    • EG: USB (Universal serial bus)

    Parallel transmission

  • Multiple bits are transferred at a time.
  • It is still used but mostly inside of computers connecting computer components.
  • It is more expensive when transferring bits at a long distance.

    • EG: IC (Integrated circuits)
